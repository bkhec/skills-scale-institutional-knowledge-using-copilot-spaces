# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers design and validate user interfaces and experiences to ensure products are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs effectively.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces, workflows, and interaction patterns
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Validate designs through user feedback and metrics
- Ensure accessibility and usability standards are met
- Maintain design systems and component libraries

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered designs that improve satisfaction and engagement
- Reduce friction and improve usability across all touchpoints
- Balance user needs with technical feasibility and business goals

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- Collaboration with Product Manager on user stories and requirements
- Daily collaboration with Developers on implementation details
- User research findings and usability test reports

### Collaboration Points
- **With Product Manager**: Aligns on user needs, business goals, and feature prioritization. Provides design insights to inform product decisions.
- **With Developers**: Collaborates on technical feasibility, implementation approach, and ensures design specifications are accurate and achievable.
- **With QA/Testing**: Defines usability requirements and participates in acceptance testing to validate user experience quality.
- **With Project Manager**: Provides design timeline estimates and flags design-related risks or dependencies.

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build, automate, and maintain delivery pipelines, infrastructure, and operational systems to enable reliable and efficient software delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure, environments, and deployment automation
- Monitor system health, performance, and availability
- Implement security best practices and compliance requirements
- Respond to incidents and perform root cause analysis
- Optimize deployment processes and infrastructure costs

### Goals
- Minimize deployment friction and time-to-production
- Ensure high system reliability, availability, and performance
- Automate repetitive tasks and improve operational efficiency
- Enable developers to ship code safely and quickly

### Typical Communication
- Deployment status updates and release coordination
- Incident response and post-mortem reports
- Infrastructure and architecture design reviews
- Operational metrics and system health dashboards

### Collaboration Points
- **With Developers**: Partners on deployment strategies, environment setup, debugging production issues, and optimizing build/test pipelines.
- **With Project Manager**: Advises on release timelines, deployment windows, environment readiness, and infrastructure-related risks.
- **With QA/Testing**: Ensures test environments mirror production and supports automated testing infrastructure.
- **With Security**: Implements security controls, monitoring, and compliance requirements in infrastructure and pipelines.

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define, collect, analyze, and interpret data to provide actionable insights that inform product decisions and measure project success.

### Responsibilities
- Define key performance indicators (KPIs) and success metrics
- Build dashboards and reports for product and business metrics
- Analyze user behavior, trends, and patterns
- Conduct A/B tests and experiments to validate hypotheses
- Identify opportunities for improvement based on data insights
- Ensure data quality and accuracy

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across the organization
- Measure impact and outcomes of product changes
- Identify trends and opportunities for improvement
- Make complex data accessible and actionable

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews and insights reports
- Experiment results and A/B test analysis
- Data requirements for new features and tracking
- Dashboard walkthroughs and metric definitions

### Collaboration Points
- **With Product Manager**: Complements product management by measuring outcomes, tracking success metrics, and identifying areas for improvement through data analysis.
- **With Developers**: Defines instrumentation requirements, works on data collection implementation, and validates tracking accuracy.
- **With Project Manager**: Provides data for progress tracking, supports retrospectives with performance insights, and measures project impact.
- **With UX Designer**: Shares user behavior data to inform design decisions and validates design improvements through metrics.

## Customer Success

### Role Summary
Customer Success represents the voice of the customer, ensuring that product decisions and features address real user needs and deliver value to customers.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and synthesize customer feedback and feature requests
- Share customer usage patterns and pain points
- Support product adoption and onboarding initiatives
- Escalate critical customer issues to appropriate teams
- Provide customer perspective during planning and prioritization
- Measure and report on customer satisfaction and retention

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction, adoption, and retention
- Ensure product improvements address real customer needs
- Reduce customer churn and support ticket volume
- Advocate for customer priorities and pain points

### Typical Communication
- Weekly customer feedback summaries
- Participation in planning and review meetings
- Customer success stories and case studies
- Escalation of high-priority customer issues

### Collaboration Points
- **With Product Manager**: Provides voice of customer during planning and review meetings. Shares feedback, usage trends, and customer priorities to inform product roadmap.
- **With Project Manager**: Helps prioritize customer-impacting work and provides customer context for project decisions.
- **With Developers**: Escalates customer issues, provides context for bug reports, and validates fixes with real customer scenarios.
- **With UX Designer**: Shares customer feedback on usability and user experience to inform design improvements.

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile processes, remove blockers, and help teams adopt and improve their development practices while upholding agile principles.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate scrum ceremonies (standups, planning, retrospectives, reviews)
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach team members on agile principles and practices
- Shield team from external disruptions
- Track team velocity and process metrics
- Foster continuous improvement and team health

### Goals
- Enable smooth, efficient team processes
- Maximize team velocity and delivery predictability
- Create a culture of continuous improvement
- Maintain healthy team dynamics and psychological safety

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of all scrum ceremonies
- One-on-one coaching with team members
- Process improvement proposals
- Metrics and velocity tracking

### Collaboration Points
- **With Project Manager**: Complements PM role with focus on team processes and agile practices. May share or divide responsibilities depending on organization structure.
- **With Developers**: Facilitates their work, removes blockers, and coaches on agile practices to improve effectiveness.
- **With Product Manager**: Ensures product backlog is well-groomed, prioritized, and ready for sprint planning.
- **With All Roles**: Works across all team members to maintain healthy team processes, facilitate communication, and drive continuous improvement.
